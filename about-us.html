About us

Hi! My name is John Marc Springael, I am an IT graduate and both the developer and owner of Kape ni ano. Kape ni ano is actually "Kape ni Manoy" since I am born and raised from Bicol. I decided to make the 'M' and 'y' from the shop name "Kape ni Manoy" silent just to make the name more interesting making it "Kape ni ano". Kape ni ano is a budget friendly coffee shop that aims to provide affordable yet good drinks for everyone. "Ang inyong suporta ay makatutulong sa pagsuporta para sa aking pamilya".
